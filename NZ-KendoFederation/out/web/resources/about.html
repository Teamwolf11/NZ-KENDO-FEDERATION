About Us

The New Zealand Kendo Federation is the governing body overseeing Kendo in New Zealand and is affiliated with the International Kendo Federation (IFK).

There are eleven affiliated clubs throughout New Zealand along with three satellite clubs. Some NZKF clubs also provide training in Naginata, Iaido and Jodo.
